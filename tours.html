Accra City Tour

A private luxury tour covering the highlights of Accra including Independence Square, Kwame Nkrumah Memorial, and Makola Market.

6–7 hrs
From $135

Tour Overview

See the best of Accra in just one day when you book this full-day sightseeing tour of the Ghanaian capital. With a private expert guide, you will visit the city’s top landmarks including Independence Square, the bustling Makola Market, and the Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park to learn about Ghana’s rich history.

This bespoke tour is fully private and customized to your pace. You will travel in an air-conditioned luxury vehicle, ensuring your comfort as we navigate the busy streets of Accra.

What's Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Expert local guide (Med or team)
  • Bottled water & Snacks
  • Lunch
  • All Entry/Admission fees

Itinerary & What to Expect

Stop 1

Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park

Visit the final resting place of Ghana's first president. Learn about his life, the fight for independence, and explore the museum housing his personal artifacts.

Stop 2

Black Star Square (Independence Square)

Explore this vast public square containing monuments to Ghana's independence struggle, including the Independence Arch and the Black Star Gate.

Stop 3

Makola Market

Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of Accra's largest open-air market. A bustling hub of trade where you can find colorful textiles, beads, and local crafts.

Stop 4

Jamestown & Ussher Fort

Walk through one of Accra's oldest districts, known for its colonial-era lighthouse, fishing harbor, and rich Ga culture.

Accra to Cape Coast Tour

A private luxury journey to the Central Region covering Slave Castles and the Kakum Park canopy walkway.

11–12 hrs
From $220

Tour Overview

Depart from Accra and head west to the Central Region, the historic heartbeat of Ghana. This private luxury tour takes you to the sobering but essential UNESCO World Heritage sites of Cape Coast and Elmina, where you will learn about the transatlantic slave trade.

Experience the journey in total comfort in our air-conditioned vehicles with a knowledgeable guide who will provide context and history throughout the trip. This tour also includes a visit to the thrilling Kakum National Park canopy walkway.

What's Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off in Accra
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Expert local guide
  • Bottled water & Snacks
  • Lunch
  • All Entry/Admission fees

Itinerary & What to Expect

Stop 1

Assin Manso Ancestral Slave River Park

Visit the site where enslaved Africans had their last bath before being marched to the coastal dungeons. A deeply moving and spiritual experience.

Stop 2

Cape Coast Castle

Tour the sprawling fortification that served as a major hub for the transatlantic slave trade. Walk through the dungeons and the infamous "Door of No Return".

Stop 3

Kakum National Park

Test your bravery by crossing the 30-meter high canopy walkway suspended in the tropical rainforest. Enjoy the lush greenery and wildlife.

$220 / per adult
  • Duration: 11-12 hours
  • Type: Private Tour
  • Free Cancellation (24 hrs)
  • Mobile Ticket Accepted

Day Tour of Cape Coast

Deep historical dive into Cape Coast Castle, Elmina Castle & nature walk at Kakum Park.

11–12 hrs
From $280

Tour Overview

This comprehensive day trip explores the twin coastal cities of Cape Coast and Elmina. We will take you through the haunting history of both Elmina Castle (the oldest European building in existence south of the Sahara) and Cape Coast Castle.

After immersing yourself in history, we shift to the beauty of Ghana's nature with a hike through Kakum National Park and its famous suspended canopy walkway.

What's Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Private transportation
  • Expert guide
  • Lunch & Refreshments
  • All admission fees

Itinerary & What to Expect

Stop 1

Elmina Castle (St. George's Castle)

Explore this 15th-century Portuguese fortification. Learn about its origins as a trade settlement and its dark pivot into a major slave holding dungeon.

Stop 2

Cape Coast Castle

A short drive away, visit the British-built Cape Coast Castle. Walk through the dungeons and reflect on the Door of No Return.

Stop 3

Kakum National Park

Experience the biodiversity of the rainforest and conquer your fear of heights on the 350-meter long canopy walkway.

$280 / per adult
  • Duration: 11-12 hours
  • Type: Day Trip
  • Free Cancellation (24 hrs)

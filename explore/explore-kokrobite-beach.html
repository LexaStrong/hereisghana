Kokrobite Beach

Beaches • 3.7 ●●●●○ (99 reviews)

About This Attraction

Kokrobite is a town along the Atlantic coast, 30 km west of Accra. It is known for traditional sea fishing, its white-sand beaches and its lively nightlife. Kokrobite is a popular destination for tourists, backpackers and international volunteers seeking beaches and a break from the busy capital city.

What to Expect

  • White Sand Beach
  • Traditional Drumming & Dancing
  • Academy of African Music and Arts
  • Beachside Nightlife

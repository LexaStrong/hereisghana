About Us

Your bespoke travel company for curated African experiences.

OUR STORY

Founded by Med

Since January 2018, Here is Ghana Tours has been creating curated African experiences for travelers seeking culture, connection, adventure, and authenticity.

We are not just a tour company; we are a bespoke travel agency that designs and executes personalized travel experiences. Led by our General Manager, Med, and an incredible team of guides and drivers, we pride ourselves on a hands-on approach. We build itineraries around each client's interests, schedule, and preferences rather than pushing fixed packages.

With a 5.0/5 average rating on TripAdvisor, our focus is on ensuring you leave Ghana feeling connected to the culture and land.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Here is Ghana Tours a pre-packaged tour company or custom?

Both! We advertise bespoke, fully custom itineraries via our direct consultations, but we also sell a set of pre-built, bookable day trips and multi-day tours that serve as great foundations for your trip.

What's included in a typical day tour?

Generally, our day tours include hotel pickup/drop-off, a private and comfortable vehicle, an expert guide, and meals (usually lunch, bottled water, and snacks like fresh coconut or mangoes). Early tours often include breakfast, and multi-day tours include dinner.

Can tours be customized?

Absolutely. This is our headline feature. We excel at last-minute additions and personalized stops to make your journey truly yours.

Do you handle group and family bookings?

Yes! Group and individual travel planning is a core service. We accommodate solo travelers, couples, families, and larger groups (like religious groups and schools).

What is your cancellation policy?

For standard tours booked through TripAdvisor, we offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the tour for a full refund. For customized, bespoke itineraries, cancellation terms may vary based on accommodations and logistics.

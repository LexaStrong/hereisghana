Cape Coast, Elmina & Kakum

A deep historical dive covering Ghana's most significant UNESCO heritage sites.

Tour Overview

This comprehensive day trip explores the twin coastal cities of Cape Coast and Elmina. We will take you through the haunting history of both Elmina Castle (the oldest European building in existence south of the Sahara) and Cape Coast Castle.

After immersing yourself in history, we shift to the beauty of Ghana's nature with a hike through Kakum National Park and its famous suspended canopy walkway.

What's Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off

  • Expert guide
  • Lunch & Refreshments
  • All admission fees

Itinerary & What to Expect

Stop 1

Elmina Castle (St. George's Castle)

Explore this 15th-century Portuguese fortification. Learn about its origins as a trade settlement and its dark pivot into a major slave holding dungeon.

Stop 2

Cape Coast Castle

A short drive away, visit the British-built Cape Coast Castle. Walk through the dungeons and reflect on the Door of No Return.

Stop 3

Kakum National Park

Experience the biodiversity of the rainforest and conquer your fear of heights on the 350-meter long canopy walkway.
